Rathinam SDG Hub

The Rathinam SDG Hub is a project led by young people, encouraging college students to contribute to the global effort for the 2030 Agenda. Through the SDG Hub Program, student volunteers become champions for sustainable development in their communities. The program recruits students for each goal, led by student leaders, who create training materials, supervise activities, and find innovative solutions to local issues. The aim is to empower students to make a positive impact and work towards a more sustainable future.
